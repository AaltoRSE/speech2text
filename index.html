Welcome to Aalto speech2text’s documentation!

Aalto speech2text is a speech recognition and diarization (speaker detection) app available to researchers of Aalto University. The list of supported languages can be found here. The app is run on Aalto Triton cluster and its usage is free for Aalto researchers.
